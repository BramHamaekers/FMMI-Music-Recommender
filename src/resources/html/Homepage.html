Welcome to our interactive music recommender

In what follows, you will be presented with a music recommender that will ask you some questions, it will then give you music recommendations based on your answers. 

When you are happy with your recommendations, you will fill in a questionnaire. Then you will go through the music recommender again, but the questions will be presented differently, followed by another questionnaire.
